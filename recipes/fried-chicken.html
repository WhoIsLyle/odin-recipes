Fried Chicken Recipe

Overview

Fried chicken is a crispy, golden-brown dish made by coating pieces of chicken in seasoned flour or batter and deep-frying them until perfectly crunchy on the outside and juicy on the inside. Its rich flavor comes from a blend of spices, while the contrast between the crisp coating and tender meat makes it a comforting and satisfying favorite enjoyed around the world.

Ingredients

Here’s a simple ingredient list with amounts for one serving (about 2 pieces) of fried chicken:

  • 2 chicken pieces (e.g., 1 drumstick + 1 thigh or breast)
  • 1/2 cup (60 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) buttermilk (or milk)
  • 1 small egg
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder (optional)
  • 1–2 cups (250–500 ml) oil for frying (enough to submerge or deep-fry)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Chicken

    • Pat the 2 chicken pieces dry with paper towels.
    • Season lightly with a pinch of salt and pepper.
    • Place them in a bowl with the buttermilk (1/2 cup).
    • Let them soak for at least 20–30 minutes (longer if you have time—up to a few hours in the fridge for better flavor and tenderness).

  2. Prepare the Coating

    3. In a separate bowl, mix:

    • 1/2 cup flour
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1/2 tsp black pepper
    • 1/2 tsp paprika
    • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
    • 1/4 tsp onion powder
    • Optional: cayenne pepper, cornstarch, baking powder

    Stir well so the spices are evenly distributed.

  3. Prepare the Egg Wash

    • In another bowl, beat 1 small egg.
    • You can mix in a splash of milk or buttermilk for a smoother consistency.

  4. Coat the Chicken

    • Remove chicken from the buttermilk (let excess drip off).
    • Dip each piece into the egg mixture.
    • Then coat thoroughly in the seasoned flour mixture, pressing the coating onto the chicken to help it stick.
    • For extra crispiness, repeat: dip again in egg → coat again in flour.

  5. Heat the Oil

    • Pour 1-2 cups of oil into a deep pan or pot (enough to cover at least half the chicken).
    • Heat to about 170-180°C (340-355°F).
    • If you don't have a thermometer, drop a bit of flour in—if it sizzles immediately, the oil is ready.

  6. Fry the Chicken

    • Carefully place the chicken into the hot oil.
    • Fry for 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally.
    • The outside should turn golden brown and crispy, and the inside fully cooked.

  7. Drain and Rest

    • Remove chicken and place on paper towels or a wire rack.
    • Let it rest for 3-5 minutes to keep it crispy.

  8. Serve

    • Serve hot with your favorite sides (fries, coleslaw, or bread).
